Check weld from section to plate, 2no 6mm fillet welds, 200mm deep.
Length of weld;	l = 200mm
Leg length of weld; 	s = 6mm
Throat length; 	a = 0.7*s 	=4.2 mm
Partial factor for shear;	γM2 = 1.25
Section ultimate stress;	fup = 470N/mm²
Correlation factor for S355;	βw = 0.9
Direction factor for a longitudinal weld;	K = 1.0
Weld shear strength;	fvwd = (fup/(3)^0.5)/(βw*γM2) 	=241.20 MPa
Weld strength per mm; 	FwTrd = a*K* fvwd 	=1.0 kN / mm
Weld strength;	Fw = FwTrd*(l-2*s)	=190.5 kN
